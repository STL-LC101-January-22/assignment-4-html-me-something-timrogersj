Harry

Harry is a 20 year old male Ball Python. He is about four feet long and likes to spend his time under a log in his cage.

Hermione

Hermione is also a 20 year old female Ball Python. She is about four and a half feet long and wants to be relaxing in a bath whenever she is not basking in her cage.

Rose

Rose is a 10 year old female Boa Constrictor. She is over seven feet long and her favorite thing to do is eat.

Lumos

Lumos is an 8 year old male cat. He let himself into my apartment when he was kitten after he was abandoned at the school across the street from my place at the time. I tried to teach him how to properly "cat" with limited success. He has been with me ever since.

Shenanigan

Shenanigan is a 7 year old female cat. I got her as a companion for Lumos after she and her littermates were dropped off at my work one day. Lumos tried to teach her how to "cat" to the best of his abilities. She is still working on it.

Goldie

Goldie is a 4 year old goldfish. I don't know if Goldie is a boy or a girl, but they are about six inches long and are pretty low maintenance.
